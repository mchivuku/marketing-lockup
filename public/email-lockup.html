Email Lock-up

Indiana University units, departments, and schools send millions of email messages a year. In order to take advantage of this marketing opportunity, we have developed a set of IU marketing lock-ups that reproduce well in the digital space.

In order to build more consistency, official email messages from Indiana University employees should include a location-specific lock-up generated by filling out the form below .

NOTE: Additions to to these (such as quotes, notes, graphics, traditional department/unit signatures) are not permitted.

Once your IU marketing lock-up is rendered below:

  1. Select using your cursor (click and drag across the entire artwork and text)
  2. Copy (command c) 
  3. Go to email client and find "signatures" in preferences
  4. Create new signature
  5. Paste (command v) into the dialog box of new email signature

THIS ARTWORK IS INTENDED FOR DIGITAL USE ONLY–QUALITY IS NOT HIGH ENOUGH FOR PRINT REPRODUCTION
