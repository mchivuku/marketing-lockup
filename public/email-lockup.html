Indiana University units, departments, and schools send millions of email messages a year. In order to take advantage of this marketing opportunity, we have developed a set of IU marketing lock-ups that reproduce well in the digital space.

In order to build more consistency, official email messages from Indiana University employees should include a location-specific lock-up generated by filling out the form below.

Additions to these (such as quotes, notes, graphics, traditional department/unit signatures) are not permitted.
